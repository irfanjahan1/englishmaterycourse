In this course, I will teach you English from the very beginner level to mastery. We will start with the basics and progress step by step.

Throughout this course, you will learn through vocabulary flashcards, phrases, sentences with translations, shadowing practice, conversations, speaking tests, assessments, and practical tasks such as recording videos of yourself.
